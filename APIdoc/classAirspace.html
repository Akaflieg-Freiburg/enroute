double Airspace::estimatedLowerBoundInFtMSL ( ) const

Estimates the lower limit of the airspace, in feet above MSL.

This method gives a rought estimate for the lower limit of the airspace in ft MSL. The result is not reliable enough for aviation purposes but can be used to sort the airspaces in the GUI.

Returns
Estimated lower bound of the airspace, in feet above main sea level
