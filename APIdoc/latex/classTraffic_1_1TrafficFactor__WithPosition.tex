\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Traffic factor whose precise position is known. 

Objects of this class represent traffic factors whose precise position is known. Other properties of the traffic, such as heading and ground speed, mifght also be known. Compared to \doxylink{classTraffic_1_1TrafficFactor__Abstract}{Traffic\+Factor\+\_\+\+Abstract}, instances of this class hold important additional property, namely the position\+Info for the traffic.
