Sense and Avoid

Midair collisions are a major reason for loss of lives in general aviation. While anti-collision systems are still not mandatory in several European countries, it is clear that no responsible pilot will fly without such a system. Enroute Flight Navigation can connect to your aircraft’s traffic receiver and show nearby traffic in the moving map.
